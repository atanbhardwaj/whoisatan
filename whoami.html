Computers

I was always smitten with these machines: the computers from when I was little, I saw those 512MB RAM computers, played around with them. Although, I did not have my own until 8th grade. But, those computers in the year 2002 - 2007 I guess. They had this huge body and seemed so attractive. I wanted to just play with them, my curiosity started to grow and since, at that time, my only option was to visit my Nani's house and that happened only when I used to get my summer vacations. My uncle would allow me to try my hands on the machine but it was only for a very short time. Nevertheless, I learned most just by looking at how they used it. Sometimes, they also did the hardware stuff too. So, from there I learned about the hardware components such as the motherboard, Hard Drive, Memory, CPU, etc. But, It never came to my mind that I would learn about computers this way, the way that I have started to learn about them right now. And yeah, now I want to use these machines in a way which would bring about a positive change in society. Contribution is my goal as of now. That's just a brief of how I was introduced to computers in my life.
